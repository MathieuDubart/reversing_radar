# Reversing radar for electric wheeling chair

### Hardware:
- 4 ultra-sound sensors
- 2 ESP 32
- LEDS

### Wiring:

Sensors Pins:

1) Trigger: 33 | Echo: 25
2) Trigger: 32 | Echo: 35
3) Trigger: 16 | Echo: 4
4) Trigger: 21 | Echo: 19

LEDs Pins:
1) Red: 22 | Green: 15
2) Red: 19 | Green: 18
3) Red: 17 | Green: 4
4) Red: 26 | Green: 14
